AXO are a set of 2,500 collectable 8bit character NFTs on Binance Smart Chain. AXO went live for sale at the rate of 1 BNB per AXO NFT. AXO is a community project, not affiliated with Binance or Binance Smart Chain.

AXO are generated pixelart GIFs and still animations, all 2,500 randomly generated with a random characteristic set. Rare traits are yet to be found, and it is all up to what the generator produced for these 2,500. People will discover rare traits over time as they analyze and review the AXO pixel art images.

The only scarcity implemented from the genesis is 10 randomly placed and generated AXO with a gold background, and the final 8 AXO have a rainbow background.
